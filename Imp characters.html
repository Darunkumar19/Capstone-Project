Important Characters in One Piece

Gol D. Roger - The legendary Pirate King who discovered the One Piece.

Whitebeard - The strongest man in the world and the man who denied the one piece .

Monkey D. Garp - A legendary Marine hero and Luffy’s grandfather.

Sengoku - The former Fleet Admiral of the Marines, known for his wisdom and strength.

Shanks - A powerful Yonko and Luffy’s mentor who gave him his iconic straw hat.

Blackbeard - A cunning and ruthless pirate who seeks ultimate power.

Five Elders - The Five Elders (Gorosei) are the top rulers of the World Government, serving Imu with immense power.

Akainu (Sakazuki) - The current Fleet Admiral, known for his extreme sense of justice.

Imu - The mysterious ruler of the world who secretly controls the world government.

Monkey D. Dragon - Monkey D. Dragon is the leader of the Revolutionary Army in One Piece and the world's most wanted man, opposing the World Government.
