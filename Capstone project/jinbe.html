Jimbie - The Helmsman

Jinbe, the helmsman of the Straw Hat Pirates, is a master of Fish-Man Karate and one of the most honorable warriors of the sea. As a former Warlord of the Sea and the ex-captain of the Sun Pirates, he has long fought for the coexistence of humans and fish-men, carrying on the will of his late captain, Fisher Tiger. Despite his serious and disciplined demeanor, Jinbe has a deep sense of loyalty and camaraderie, fully dedicating himself to any cause he believes in. His ultimate dream is to see a world where fish-men are treated as equals, free from discrimination and oppression.

As a fish-man, Jinbe possesses immense strength, able to overpower even the toughest enemies with his mastery of Fish-Man Karate and Fish-Man Jujutsu. His combat skills allow him to manipulate water as if it were a weapon, making him extremely dangerous both on land and in the sea. As the crew’s helmsman, his incredible skill at steering the Thousand Sunny makes him indispensable, especially in the unpredictable waters of the Grand Line. His vast experience as a former pirate captain and a Warlord provides the crew with tactical wisdom, making him one of the most level-headed members of the Straw Hats.

Jinbe’s accomplishments throughout his journey have been nothing short of legendary. He played a vital role in the Summit War, helping Luffy escape Marineford and guiding him through his grief after Ace’s death. During the Whole Cake Island arc, he fearlessly declared his allegiance to Luffy, standing up to Big Mom and ensuring the crew’s safe escape. In Wano, he officially joined the Straw Hats and showcased his strength by defeating Who’s-Who, a former CP9 agent and one of the Tobiroppo. His leadership, battle prowess, and unwavering loyalty make him one of the most formidable members of the Straw Hat Pirates, bringing them one step closer to their ultimate goal.
